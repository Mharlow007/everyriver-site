EveryRiver Atlas Limited

EveryRiver Atlas is the not-for-profit organisation behind the initiative. Every penny it receives in income (which is so far none, if you're wondering) can only be spent on the following objectives:

Objects

The Company is established for purposes that are exclusively not-for-profit and for the public benefit:

  1. To advance public understanding of rivers, canals, lakes, wetlands, aquifers, estuaries and related environments - covering scientific, cultural, historical and social aspects - by collecting, curating and communicating knowledge and data through storytelling, interpretation, publications (including digital), exhibitions, performances, broadcasts and other educational activities.
  2. To increase responsible use and enjoyment and informed stewardship of those water environments by sharing knowledge, disseminating useful research and creating openly-licensed information resources and datasets that help people see, understand and value these places.
  3. To build community participation by enabling volunteering and collaborative projects that gather, verify and share knowledge (including contributions to open platforms such as encyclopaedias and public datasets), and by offering training that supports those aims.
  4. To collect, commission and exhibit creative works that interpret and celebrate these environments where this advances the purposes above.
  5. To pursue such other purposes as are exclusively charitable according to the law of England and Wales and are connected with the preceding objects.

Privacy notice

EveryRiver respects your privacy and only collects the information needed to run the project and keep in touch with interested supporters.

What information we collect

If you sign up for updates, we collect your email address.

How we use your information

We use your email address to send occasional updates about EveryRiver, such as new maps, features, discoveries, and opportunities to give feedback or help shape the project.

We do not use your email for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Lawful basis

We process your email address on the basis of consent. You give this consent when you voluntarily sign up for updates via the EveryRiver website.

Emails and unsubscribing

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can unsubscribe at any time, and your email address will be removed from our mailing list.

Data processing

Emails are sent using MailerLite, which acts as a data processor on our behalf. MailerLite processes data in accordance with GDPR and only for the purpose of sending emails for EveryRiver.

How long we keep your data

We keep your email address only for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your details are deleted or anonymised.

Your rights

Under data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Ask for your data to be corrected or deleted
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy notice or how your data is used, you can contact us at: hello@every-river.org
