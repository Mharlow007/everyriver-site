For the love of rivers everywhere

We believe the more you know a river, the more you love it; enjoy it; care for it; have fun. And from that comes a deeper curiosity. We come together to spark that interest and quench the thirst to know more.

This is not a spectator sport

We need you. This is not just for you. This is by you. EveryRiver is a not for profit organisation that only exists to enable people to come together to tell stories and grow people's undertanding and enjoyment of, well, EveryRiver everywhere. That breathtaking ambition may never truly be achieved, but, wow, imagine if we got close. Join the flow.
